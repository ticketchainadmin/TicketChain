1. Eligibility

Participation in TicketChain lottery draws is open to individuals aged 18 years or older. By purchasing a ticket, you confirm that you meet this age requirement and that lottery participation is legal in your jurisdiction.

  • Employees and affiliates of TicketChain are not eligible to participate.
  • Participants must have a valid cryptocurrency wallet address.
  • Only one account per person is permitted.

2. Ticket Purchases

All ticket purchases are final and non-refundable once payment has been submitted. Tickets are valid only for the specific draw they were purchased for. Ticket IDs are generated at the time of purchase and serve as your proof of entry.

  • Maximum 10 tickets per person per draw unless otherwise stated.
  • Special Edition draws may have different purchase limits.
  • Tickets are non-transferable.

3. Draws & Winners

All draws are conducted using a provably fair blockchain-based random selection process. Results are final and cannot be contested. Winners are notified via the wallet address provided at time of purchase.
